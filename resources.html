Resources

The open access resource library initiative promotes equal access to high school course notes for students. We accept notes donations all year round for all subject areas and school districts.

Open Access Library

Student volunteers provide free ESL lessons to children internationally and new immigrants in Canada through our organization. This initiative operates on a "pay-it-forward" model where graduates of the program become future ESL lesson facilitators.

ESL Classes

Our team frequently hosts guest speaker and panel events with speakers from a variety of educational and socio-economic backgrounds. Recently, we hosted a university student panel for highschoolers to get more insight into different programs and educational opportunities.
